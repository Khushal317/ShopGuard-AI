# ShopGuard Store Policies

## Shipping

Standard shipping usually arrives within 3 to 5 business days after the order leaves the warehouse. Expedited shipping usually arrives within 1 to 2 business days after the order leaves the warehouse.

Orders are normally processed within 1 business day. Orders placed after 3 PM local warehouse time begin processing the next business day.

## Returns

Customers may request a return within 14 days of delivery. Returned items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached.

Final sale items, gift cards, and items marked as non-returnable are not eligible for return.

## Refunds

Refunds are reviewed after the returned item is received and inspected. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item arrived damaged or the wrong item was sent.
